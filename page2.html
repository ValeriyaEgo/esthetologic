FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions that clients may have when visiting a beauty salon

WHAT SHOULD I DO TO PREPARE FOR MY SALON APPOINTMENT?

It's best to arrive with clean, dry hair and a clean face (if applicable). Avoid applying heavy products or styling your hair excessively beforehand, as it can make it more challenging for the stylist to work with your hair effectively.

HOW FAR IN ADVANCE SHOULD I BOOK MY APPOINTMENT?

It's recommended to book your appointment at least a week in advance to ensure availability, especially for popular time slots or specific stylists. However, it's always a good idea to call the salon directly for their specific booking guidelines.

WHAT IF I'M UNSURE ABOUT THE HAIRSTYLE OR COLOR I WANT?

Don't worry! The salon professionals are there to assist you. They can provide guidance based on your preferences, face shape, and hair type. Bring reference pictures or consult with your stylist for suggestions and ideas.

HOW LONG WILL MY APPOINTMENT TAKE?

The duration of your appointment depends on the services you've booked. Haircuts typically take about 30 minutes to an hour, while color treatments or complex styles may take several hours. It's best to inquire about the estimated time when booking your appointment.

HOW OFTEN SHOULD I GET A HAIRCUT OR COLOR TOUCH-UP?

The frequency of haircuts and color touch-ups varies based on individual hair growth and the desired style. On average, haircuts are recommended every 4-8 weeks, while color touch-ups can range from 4-12 weeks, depending on the color and regrowth.
