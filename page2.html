Skin Whitening FAQ

HOW  MANY TREATMENTS ARE NECESSARY?

Typically, a course of 3-5 treatments is required, depending on your skin type, condition, and desired goals.

IS THERE A HOMECARE ROUTINE REQUIRED?

Following the aftercare instructions and using recommended homecare products for at least 6 months is essential. Regular reviews and touch-up sessions with your skin therapist will monitor progress.

WHAT CAN I EXPECT AFTER TREATMENT?

You may experience redness, swelling for about 48 hours, and flaky skin in the days following treatment, which is normal.

CONTRAINDICATIONS

Pregnancy, breastfeeding, aspirin allergy, dermatitis, sunburn, recent sun exposure, open lesions, skin infections, photosensitivity.

PRODUCTS TO AVOID ONE WEEK BEFORE AND AFTER TREATMENT?

- AHA's or Retinoids (Vitamin A)
- Direct sun exposure
- Alpha/Beta hydroxy acids
- Glycolic, lactic, salicylic acids (not limited)
- Benzoyl peroxide (Epiduo)/adapalene (Differin)
- Hydroquinone/kojic acid/azelaic acids
